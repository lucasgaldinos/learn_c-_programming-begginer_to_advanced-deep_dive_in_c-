# Bitwise

the bitwise operators are:

- `&` bitwise and
- `|` bitwise or
- `^` bitwise xor
- `~` bitwise not
- `<<` bitwise left shift
- `>>` bitwise right shift

| operator | example | result |
|----------|---------|--------|
| `&`      | `0b1010 & 0b1100` | `0b1000` |
| `\|`      | `0b1010 \| 0b1100` | `0b1110` |
| `^`      | `0b1010 ^ 0b1100` | `0b0110` |
| `~`      | `~0b1010` | `0b0101` |
| `<<`     | `0b1010 << 1` | `0b10100` |
| `>>`     | `0b1010 >> 1` | `0b0101` |
